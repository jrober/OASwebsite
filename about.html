About Open Access Solar

Mission Statement

Provide accessible, sustainable energy to the general public by making solar technology available in public spaces to charge portable electronics.

Objectives

  • Implement solar cells in various public spaces and determine if the energy produced is both cost effective and actively used by the community.
    • Public spaces of interest include outdoor benches, tables, buses, bus stops, blinds, posters, and airports. 
    • These locations are to be where people actively gather and need to charge their devices.
  • Provide portable electronics with energy that is readily available and sustainable
  • Convert small energy savings into major energy savings as the community becomes more self-reliant and invested in environmental friendliness.
  • Branch out of BYU and make an impact in public schools and cities.
  • Aid in decreasing pollution and increase the use of renewable energy resources.
  • Help developing countries around the world by providing them with sustainable energy.
